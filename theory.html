Introduction

The order of a differential equation is the highest degree of derivative present in the given equation. A system in which the input-output equation is a second order differential equation is called Second Order System.

There are certain factors that make second order systems important. They are very simple and exhibit oscillations and overshoot.All types of higher order systems are based on second order systems. In case of mechanical second order systems, energy is stored in the form of inertia whereas in case of electrical systems, energy can be stored in a capacitor or inductor.
